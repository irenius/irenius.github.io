Dorunner

Dorunner, this is where I spend most my hours. It's a place where, for example, someone who needs to build a new porch can get in touch with carpenters. Free, fast and they get quotes from companies who are interested in doing the job.

The site is available in Sweden, Denmark and Norway. There is a lot more than this public site. There is login-required site for connected companies, a login-required site for people who need help with something, where you can manage your requests. And of course and admin page, where the companies and requests are handled.

I´ve been with building all parts of the site, from database to CSS.

  • Languages: PHP (Laravel), MySQL, JQuery, AngularJS
